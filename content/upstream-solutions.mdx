<h1>Industry Solutions for Upstream Oil &amp; Gas</h1>

Sierra Digital provides gold standard application extensions for popular Upstream Oil & Gas modules like SAP Joint Venture Accounting (SAP JVA), SAP Production Revenue Accounting (SAP PRA), and Upstream Operations Management (SAP UOM).

<a href="/contact/">Get Started</a>

Sierra’s solution experts are industry leaders in Upstream Oil & Gas and our sister company, Stonebridge Consulting, has an extensive history in the industry. We maintain an innovative IP of industry solutions, including multiple apps built on SAP BTP platform. Sierra is proud to have worked with multiple Tier 1 Oil & Gas companies to review process automations, advanced analytics and AI services in support of a Clean Core delivery model.

<h2>Our Platform Managed Services Models</h2>

Sierra’s SAP BTP solutions extend the functionality of existing SAP solutions to ensure the day-to-day requirements of the Upstream Oil & Gas customers are met.

<h3>Business Partner Portal</h3>

<p>
  The portal provides self-service access to owners to request updates to contact, banking and
  payment preference information. Additionally, revenue ownership and payment details as well as JIB
  Ownership and invoice history data are available for review and download to MS Excel. Integrations
  with support ticket systems, document management applications and DocuSign are also available
  within the portal solution.
</p>

<h3>Payout Management</h3>

<p>
  The Payout solution delivers an innovative approach to managing simple or complex scenarios
  related to the term as and provisions of owner payout calculations and reporting. Trusted SAP APIs
  deliver the ownership, expense and revenue data required to manage the payout calculations, owner
  reporting and payout projections.
</p>

<h3>Capital Projects &amp; AFE</h3>

<p>
  The Capital Project Builder solution delivers a streamlined approach to managing internal project
  budgets, WBS element templates, workflows, approvals and analytics. SAP EPPM and Project System
  (PS) integrations extend the functionality of SAP. A natural extension of the solution now
  includes comprehensive AFE budgeting, workflow approvals, owner balloting and integration with SAP
  for cash call management. Powerful analytics drive the solution to new levels.
</p>

<h3>Budget, Forecast &amp; Planning with SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC)</h3>

<p>
  The solution delivers powerful tools utilizing SAP PRA and JVA data for LOS analytics. SAC data
  actions deliver extensive options for projections based on production volumes, pricing, inflation
  rates, etc. The solution allows for a “story-telling” approach to deliver multiple views of
  potential scenarios related to the development, acquisition or divestiture of assets while
  allowing a user-defined hierarchy of assets to be maintained. Multi-currency support is also
  available.
</p>

<h3>Revenue Data &amp; Process Analytics</h3>

<p>
  This solution offers intuitive interfaces for the review of Venture distributions, Combined Run
  analysis, Ownership Transfer (chain of title) analysis and Escheat analytics.
</p>

<h3>Volumetric Data Analytics</h3>

<p>
  This application is designed to assist in the management of exceptions within the volume
  allocation and reporting aspect of the business. Downtime and shut-in analytics allow production
  technicians to easily identify issues that affect daily and monthly production outlooks. Oil, gas
  and water production and product sales analytics enhance the use of the UOM and PRA solutions.
</p>
