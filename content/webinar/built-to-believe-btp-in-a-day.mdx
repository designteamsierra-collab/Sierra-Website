<h2>About the Workshop</h2>

<p>
  This is a fully hands-on day. No passive demos – just you, your team, and your own BTP tenant.
  Sierra will guide you through two powerful tracks across the day: the morning explores what’s
  possible with BTP Build and AI services, and the afternoon puts the power of SAP’s generative AI
  copilot in your hands through custom Joule skill development.
</p>

<p>
  By the time you leave, you won’t just understand what BTP can do – you’ll have built something
  with it.
</p>

<h4>Session 1 - Morning</h4>

<h2>Workflow Automation with BTP</h2>

<p>
  AI Harness the intelligence of BTP’s AI services to automate and streamline enterprise workflows.
</p>

<h4>Session 2 - Afternoon</h4>

<h2>
  SAP Build and <br /> Work Zone
</h2>

Create unified, role-based digital workspaces and low-code applications with SAP Build.

<h4>Session 3 - Afternoon</h4>

<h2>Custom Joule Skill Development</h2>

Extend SAP’s generative AI copilot with custom skills tailored to your own business processes.

<h4>Morning track | 8:30 AM to 12:00 PM</h4>

<h2>BTP Build and AI</h2>

Discover the breadth of BTP’s Build and AI services, get hands-on in your own tenant, and experience the power of intelligent automation in the SAP ecosystem.

<h4>Afternoon track | 1:00 PM to 4:30 PM</h4>

<h2>Custom Joule Skill Development</h2>

Go beyond out-of-the-box AI. Build and deploy custom skills for Joule, SAP’s generative AI copilot, extending its intelligence to fit your unique workflows.
