<h1>Supercharge Change Management with AI-Driven Digital Adoption</h1>

<p>
  The AI tech train is moving fast. Is your organization’s digital adoption keeping up? Join Sierra
  Digital and WalkMe for a live, demo-packed session on how AI-infused guidance is transforming the
  way enterprises manage change – and unlocking faster ROI from SAP investments.
</p>

<p>
  📅 Wednesday, March 25, 2026 <br />
  🕚 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM CT <br />
  🎙 Free to attend | Live + Q&amp;A

<h2>What This Session is About</h2>

<p>
  Every SAP transformation reaches the same inflection point: the technology is live, but the people
  aren’t quite there yet. Support tickets climb. Super users stretch thin. And the ROI that
  justified the investment starts to feel further away than it should.
</p>

<p>
  This webinar tackles that problem directly. Sierra Digital and WalkMe are bringing together two
  practitioners who work at the intersection of AI, change management, and SAP adoption every day –
  for an honest, practical conversation about what actually works.
</p>

<p>
  In this session, we’ll explore how organizations can close the gap between deployment and true
  adoption using AI-driven digital adoption strategies. You’ll gain practical insights on reducing
  support dependency, empowering users, and accelerating the value of your SAP investment.
</p>
