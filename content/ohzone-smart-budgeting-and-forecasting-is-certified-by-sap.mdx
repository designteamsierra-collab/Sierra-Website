<h1>OhZone Smart Budgeting and Forecasting is certified by SAP</h1>

<a href="/contact/">Get Started</a>

<p>
  <strong>Houston — June 26, 2024 —</strong> Sierra Digital announced today that its exclusive
  offering, OhZone Smart Budgeting and Forecasting (
  <strong>
    <a href="/ohzone/">OhZone SBF</a>
  </strong>
  ), is certified by SAP as built on SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP), SAP’s platform for
  the Intelligent Enterprise.
</p>

<p>
  “OhZone Smart Budgeting and Forecasting turns outputs into outcomes, livening user data with
  predictive/prescriptive analytics while maximizing the impact of diverse strategic initiatives.
  And now, upgraded with AI functionality, OhZoneSBF boasts all the automation tools that our
  customers need to win the race towards AI advantages,” said Senthil Kumar, CEO at Sierra Digital.
</p>

<p>
  OhZoneSBF, now available on the{" "}
  <strong>
    <a href="https://store.sap.com/dcp/en/product/display-2001011442_live_v1/ohzone-smart-budgeting-forecasting">
      SAP Store
    </a>
  </strong>
  , is a favored solution among the OhZone app suite, a family of BTP-based app frameworks that
  breathe life into business processes, enhancing outcomes and driving efficiency, with automation
  and analytics tools. OhZoneSBF, built on SAP BTP, is packed with time-saving features that make
  budgeting easy. And, through its integration with{" "}
  <strong>
    <a href="/sap-analytics-cloud/">SAP Analytics Cloud</a>
  </strong>
  , OhZoneSBF can confidently tailor budgets to the evolving priorities of unique businesses.
</p>

<p>
  SAP Integration and Certification Center (SAP ICC) has certified that the integration software for
  OhZone Smart Budgeting and Forecasting is built on SAP BTP. SAP BTP helps companies connect and
  integrate their business processes and data with SAP and third-party applications to make
  well-informed decisions and meet their evolving needs.
</p>

<p>
  <strong>About Sierra:</strong> Sierra Digital is SAP’s BTP and AI Innovation Station, connecting
  enterprise users to revolutionary technologies that simplify, enhance, and extend SAP system value
  without the time, cost, or risk of traditional development cycles. Sierra is a global partner to
  SAP, providing quick Cloud advantages with an SAP-certified “follow-the-sun” model comprised of
  low-code/no-code app development and expert adoption support. Privately held and headquartered in
  Houston, Sierra employs more than 300 in the United States and abroad.
</p>
