<h2>The BW Journey</h2>

<p>
  90% of the world still runs on-premise – SAP Business Warehouse, SAP’s favored data-warehousing
  product, houses an unimaginable quantity of vital business data. But SAP BW has already entered
  its end-of-life stages, and SAP will officially terminate maintenance for SAP BW in 2027.
</p>

<p>
  The clock is ticking: SAP users, en masse, have to begin migrating their systems to the cloud in a
  few months. But cloud migration isn’t always speedy. Most often, this intensive transformation
  demands an 18-month implementation window. And before migration can start, business have to
  budget, plan, survey their existing systems, and ask questions about their goals for cloud
  migration. SAP Business Warehouse is a robust system containing a large number of modeling, ETL,
  and reporting elements. Assessing the current state of the system is a cumbersome process to
  undertake manually. Migration, with all the planning and preparation it requires, is a long
  process, but users are running out of time to migrate their SAP BW systems to the cloud.
</p>

<h3>Migration Made Easy</h3>

<p>
  But Sierra Digital makes migration easy with the{" "}
  <a href="https://store.sap.com/dcp/en/product/display-2002001013_live_v1/infocus-data-readiness-assessment">
    <strong>InFocus Data Readiness Assessment</strong>
  </a>
  , a migration accelerator{" "}
  <a href="https://www.sap.com/registration/free-assessment-offer-for-bw-customers.html">
    <strong>officially sponsored</strong>
  </a>{" "}
  by SAP. Using either the ABAP Adaptor or HANA Adaptor, which are well-established methods for
  connecting to SAP Systems, Sierra establishes a remote connection to your SAP BW system. The
  InFocus Data Assessment then scans the metadata of your SAP BW System, ensuring that there is no
  disruption to live business operations. Sierra does not access or work with live Production Data,
  ensuring that there is no disruption to live business operations.
</p>

<p>
  The Assessment tool generates essential components crucial for making informed decisions about the
  migration, including identifying what data needs to be migrated, how it should be transformed, and
  how it can be visualized. The analysis provides insights about the past and current use of BW
  Objects. Users benefit from an ability to understand objects that are in high use versus objects
  that are dormant or unused. Decision-makers therefore gain insight on BW object management,
  enhancement prioritization, and overall data strategy alignment. The assessment examines data
  volume, data model complexity, calculation logic, and dependencies in order to guide
  decision-making for object maintenance, optimization efforts, and resource allocation for future
  development and support. With the InFocus Data Assessment, users receive object inventories,
  overviews of as-is and to-be architectures, cost and time estimators, and more.
</p>

<p>
  All data and component detail generated during the Assessment is seamlessly integrated with SAP
  Analytics Cloud (SAC). Integration allows for the data to be presented in an easy-to-read format
  with insightful visuals and natural language queries powered by SAC Just Ask.
</p>

<h3>Why Datasphere?</h3>

<p>
  Migrating from SAP BW to SAP Datasphere in the cloud offers substantial benefits to enterprises.
  By leveraging real-time analytics through SAP Analytics Cloud, companies can make quicker, more
  informed decisions. SAP Analytics Cloud Integrations provide business users with actionable
  insights through dynamic visualizations. Modernizing an outdated data and analytics foundation
  enables organizations to drive innovation, reduce costs, and maintain a competitive edge. With
  Sierra’s expert guidance, businesses can fully exploit the insights within their data, advancing
  towards becoming intelligent enterprises.
</p>

<p>
  And Datasphere provides the semantic-rich, contextualized data fabric that your organization needs
  to take full advantage of the AI revolution. Data needs context for authoritative decision making.
  GenAI can be the next revolution in productivity, but it requires contextualized, high-quality
  data – with centralized data governance and simplified cloud landscapes. Datasphere allows you to
  take full advantage of both SAP and non-SAP data – stored in different applications across the
  enterprise – to increase productivity, expand insight, and drive value.
</p>

<p>
  And Datasphere effortlessly scales to manage increasing data volumes and complexity, providing
  organizations with the flexibility needed to grow and seize new opportunities. By unifying
  disparate systems into a single source of truth, businesses reduce data redundancy and enhance
  consistency.
</p>
