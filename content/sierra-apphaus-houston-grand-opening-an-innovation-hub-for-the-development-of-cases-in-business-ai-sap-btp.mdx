<h2>Sierra AppHaus Houston Grand Opening – an Innovation Hub</h2>

<a href="/contact/">Get Started</a>

<h1>
  Sierra AppHaus Houston Grand Opening – an Innovation Hub for the development of cases in Business
  AI &amp; SAP BTP
</h1>

<p>
  Sierra was joined by our customers, partners, and friends to celebrate the inauguration of{" "}
  <strong>
    <a href="https://apphaus.sap.com/network/houston">Sierra AppHaus Houston</a>
  </strong>
  , launched in collaboration with the AppHaus program at SAP.
</p>

<p>
  Chosen for our expertise in Business AI and SAP BTP, our deep industry experience in Oil &amp;
  Gas, and our world-class facilities,{" "}
  <strong>Sierra is now the 2nd US partner to join the SAP AppHaus network</strong>. We are proud to
  introduce this new resource for customer success, and we look forward to deploying the unique
  AppHaus methodology for design-thinking workshops in this beautiful new space.
</p>

<p>
  We are proud to be part of the SAP AppHaus Network and to contribute our design and innovation
  expertise, said <strong>Senthil Kumar, CEO and Chairman of Sierra Digital</strong>.
</p>

<p>
  <em>
    “This collaboration with SAP allows us to co-create impactful solutions that accelerate digital
    transformation for our clients and strengthen our regional presence.”
  </em>
</p>

<p>
  <strong>Carlos Estala Velasco, Co-Lead SAP AppHaus Partner Network</strong>, says:“
  <em>
    Sierra Digital has been recognized as a perfect addition to the SAP AppHaus Network. With a
    committed AppHaus team equipped to apply our award-winning human-centered innovation approach,
    they are able to inspire and support customers throughout their journey to realize innovation.
    We eagerly anticipate co-innovating with the local team!
  </em>
  ”
</p>
