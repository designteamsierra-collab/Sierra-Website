<h1>SAP BTP &amp; AI Innovation Day</h1>

<p>Hosted at Sierra AppHaus – Houston</p>

<p>November 5, 2025</p>

<p>9:00 AM – 5:00 PM For Energy &amp; Utilities Innovators</p>

<p>
  Join SAP and Sierra Digital for an exclusive, full-day customer-only experience designed to
  explore the real-world impact of SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP), Embedded AI, and Clean
  Core strategies across the Energy &amp; Utilities landscape.
</p>

<p>
  This event brings together industry leaders, SAP executives, and peers for a high-impact session —
  combining strategic insights, interactive demos, and a hands-on design thinking workshop in the
  award-winning Sierra AppHaus.
</p>
