<h2>
  Sierra Digital and UiPath Celebrate Five Years of Partnership Delivering Business Outcome–Focused
  Automation Solutions to the Energy Industry
</h2>

<p>
  Strategic collaboration drives operational efficiency, SAP ERP optimization, and measurable value
  for energy enterprises
</p>

[Houston, Texas] – February 21, 2026 – Sierra Digital and UiPath today announced the celebration of their five-year strategic partnership, marking a significant milestone in delivering intelligent automation solutions tailored to the energy industry. Over the past half decade, the two organizations have worked together to help energy companies accelerate digital transformation, optimize SAP ERP environments, and achieve measurable business outcomes.

<h2>Driving Measurable Business Outcomes</h2>

Together, Sierra Digital and UiPath have enabled clients to:

<ul>
  {" "}
  <li>Streamline finance, supply chain, asset management, and regulatory processes</li>{" "}
  <li>Enhance data accuracy and compliance across SAP ERP environments</li>{" "}
  <li>Reduce manual effort and operational costs</li>{" "}
  <li>Improve cycle times for critical business functions</li>{" "}
  <li>Increase visibility and decision-making agility</li>{" "}
</ul>

<h2>SAP ERP Optimization Through Intelligent Automation</h2>

Through this partnership, Sierra Digital has developed SAP-centric automation accelerators and frameworks powered by UiPath to:

<ul>
  {" "}
  <li>Automate high-volume transactional processes</li> <li>Improve master data governance</li>{" "}
  <li>Enable touchless invoice processing and reconciliations</li>{" "}
  <li>Support outage management and asset lifecycle workflows</li>{" "}
  <li>Strengthen compliance and reporting capabilities</li>{" "}
</ul>

This integrated approach ensures that automation initiatives are aligned with enterprise strategy and deliver tangible return on investment.

<h2>Supporting the Energy Industry’s Digital Evolution</h2>

As the energy sector continues to navigate market volatility, regulatory pressure, sustainability initiatives, and workforce transformation, intelligent automation has become a critical enabler of resilience and scalability.

Over the past five years, Sierra Digital and UiPath have partnered to deliver scalable automation programs that support digital transformation roadmaps across upstream, midstream, downstream, and utilities organizations.
