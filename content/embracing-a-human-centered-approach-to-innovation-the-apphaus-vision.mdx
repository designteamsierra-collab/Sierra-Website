<h1>ANNOUNCING SIERRA APPHAUS HOUSTON</h1>

<strong>January 28, 2025</strong>

<a href="/contact/">Get Started</a>

<h1>Embracing a Human-Centered Approach to Innovation: The AppHaus Vision</h1>

At Sierra Digital, our commitment to innovation is a fundamental aspect of our mission. We are thrilled to announce that we are bringing this commitment to the next level with the inauguration of our very own

<strong>
  <a href="https://apphaus.sap.com/network">AppHaus</a>
</strong>

, set to launch on

<strong>January 28, 2025</strong>

. This dynamic space is more than just an office or a meeting room—it’s a vibrant ecosystem designed to foster collaboration, creativity, and problem-solving through a Human-Centered approach to innovation, leveraging the capabilities of SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP).

In today’s fast-evolving world, relying solely on tech solutions is insufficient. The most effective innovations begin with a profound understanding of people—be it customers, employees, or stakeholders. A Human-Centered approach to innovation ensures that the needs, desires, and challenges of are prioritized throughout the problem-solving process. This methodology results in solutions that are not only technologically advanced but also intuitive, impactful paving the way for significant change.

Sierra Digital’s AppHaus will embody this philosophy, acting as a space where businesses can step into their users’ perspectives and design solutions that truly make a difference.

<h2>What to Expect from AppHaus</h2>

<p>
  The Sierra Digital AppHaus will provide a collaborative environment where teams can engage
  directly with our experts in a setting tailored for brainstorming, testing, and refining ideas.
  AppHaus invites SAP users from all lines of business to share their diverse experiences to the
  invention of that enhance efficiency and humanity in business processes.
</p>

<p>
  The SAP AppHaus program has pioneered the Human-Centered Approach to Innovation (HCAI), grounded
  in research from global innovation experts. This proven methodology places SAP users in an
  environment conducive to free thinking. In this space, dynamic and gamified workshops stimulate
  new ideas that are evaluated, tested, and implemented by a team of functional, technical, and
  design specialists at Sierra.
</p>

<p>
  Establishing the AppHaus marks not just an advancement for Sierra Digital but a significant leap
  forward. We are reaffirming our dedication to placing people at the core of our initiatives. With
  this new space, we aspire to unite the SAP community for ongoing collaboration as we collectively
  forge a path toward future innovations.
</p>

<h3>Join Us</h3>

<p>
  We invite you to celebrate the official opening of Sierra AppHaus Houston on January 28, 2025.
  This informal gathering will feature speeches from SAP leadership and experts from Sierra Digital.
</p>
